Acceptance

Category: Personal

The willingness to embrace what is, rather than what should be or might have been. It involves making peace with yourself, others, and life's imperfections.

Example in action

When a sudden career change closed one door, Maya practiced acceptance by honoring what had ended, then confidently explored new paths, discovered fresh strengths, and built an even more meaningful life.
