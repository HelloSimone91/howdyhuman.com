Justice

Category: Core Values

Fair treatment for everyone, ensuring that rights are respected and wrongs are corrected.

Why this matters

Justice matters because the idea of "Fair treatment for everyone, ensuring that rights are respected and wrongs are corrected" is only trustworthy when it changes behavior. It helps define what kind of person, team, or community someone is becoming. You can often see it in the decision to promote, especially when nobody is forcing that choice. This gives justice enough specificity to be noticed in a conversation, routine, decision, or repair.

Examples in action

  • The community coalition advanced justice by organizing legal aid, tenant education, and policy advocacy to protect families from unfair evictions.
  • When convenience starts to pull attention away from justice, the value becomes concrete by returning to "defend" as a next move.
  • When the moment is messy, justice may look like choosing to promote before the perfect language, timing, or permission arrives.

How to practice

  • Choose one related value, such as Advocacy, Bravery, and Courage, and decide whether the moment needs that value, justice, or both.
  • Compare justice with Advocacy, Bravery, and Courage; notice where they support each other and where they ask for different actions.
  • Reread the opening example and name the exact behavior that made justice visible.
  • Name the pressure that makes justice harder, then choose one action that answers that pressure directly.

Frequently asked questions

What does justice mean as a value?
Justice is not only a word for Fair treatment for everyone, ensuring that rights are respected and wrongs are corrected; it is also a pattern of behavior shaped by actions like promote, defend, uphold, and advocate.
How do I practice justice?
Make justice practical by pairing it with a small commitment: one choice to uphold, one conversation to have, or one pattern to interrupt.
What values are related to justice?
Looking at Advocacy, Bravery, and Courage alongside justice can make the choice in front of someone more precise.
