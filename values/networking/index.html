Networking

Category: Interpersonal

Building and maintaining relationships with people to share information and opportunities.

Why this matters

Networking turns "Building and maintaining relationships with people to share information and opportunities" into a lived value by giving it a form other people can notice. As an interpersonal value, it shapes the space between people. It often shows up through the willingness to connect before the situation is neat or fully rewarded. Without that behavioral edge, networking can stay vague even when the word feels important.

Examples in action

  • She approached networking as relationship-building rather than transaction, focusing on how she could help others and creating genuine connections.
  • When the moment is messy, networking may look like choosing to connect before the perfect language, timing, or permission arrives.
  • Networking can be practiced quietly, too, through the choice to link without turning the action into proof of identity.

How to practice

  • Reread the opening example and name the exact behavior that made networking visible.
  • Turn the definition into a sentence that starts with "Today I will..." and includes one concrete behavior.
  • Make the value visible to one other person through a choice they could actually notice.
  • After acting, ask whether someone else could have observed networking in what you actually did.

Frequently asked questions

What does networking mean as a value?
Networking becomes a value when Building and maintaining relationships with people to share information and opportunities starts influencing what someone chooses, refuses, repairs, or repeats.
How do I practice networking?
Choose a situation where the value has been mostly theoretical. Then make it visible through one action connected to connect, link, relate, and communicate.
What values are related to networking?
Networking often connects with Approachability, Camaraderie, and Charisma. Those values can support networking, sharpen its meaning, or reveal a tension worth noticing.
