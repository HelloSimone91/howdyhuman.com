Spirituality

Category: Core Values

A deep connection to something greater than oneself, often involving personal beliefs and practices that provide meaning and purpose in life.

Why this matters

Spirituality matters because the idea of "A deep connection to something greater than oneself, often involving personal beliefs and practices that provide meaning and purpose in life" is only trustworthy when it changes behavior. It helps define what kind of person, team, or community someone is becoming. You can often see it in the decision to connect, especially when nobody is forcing that choice. This gives spirituality enough specificity to be noticed in a conversation, routine, decision, or repair.

Examples in action

  • She nurtured her spirituality through regular meditation, time in nature, and participating in a community that shared her belief in interconnectedness.
  • When convenience starts to pull attention away from spirituality, the value becomes concrete by returning to "explore" as a next move.
  • When the moment is messy, spirituality may look like choosing to connect before the perfect language, timing, or permission arrives.

How to practice

  • Choose one related value, such as Approachability, Camaraderie, and Charisma, and decide whether the moment needs that value, spirituality, or both.
  • Compare spirituality with Approachability, Camaraderie, and Charisma; notice where they support each other and where they ask for different actions.
  • Reread the opening example and name the exact behavior that made spirituality visible.
  • Name the pressure that makes spirituality harder, then choose one action that answers that pressure directly.

Frequently asked questions

What does spirituality mean as a value?
Spirituality is not only a word for A deep connection to something greater than oneself, often involving personal beliefs and practices that provide meaning and purpose in life; it is also a pattern of behavior shaped by actions like connect, explore, seek, and practice.
How do I practice spirituality?
Make spirituality practical by pairing it with a small commitment: one choice to seek, one conversation to have, or one pattern to interrupt.
What values are related to spirituality?
Looking at Approachability, Camaraderie, and Charisma alongside spirituality can make the choice in front of someone more precise.
