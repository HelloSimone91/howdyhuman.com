Sports

Category: Personal

Activities that involve physical exertion and skill, often played individually or in teams for competition or enjoyment.

Why this matters

Sports matters when "Activities that involve physical exertion and skill, often played individually or in teams for competition or enjoyment" becomes a practice someone can return to under pressure. As a personal value, it steadies the relationship between intention and behavior. One signal is the choice to play when a more automatic reaction would be easier. The goal is to make sports concrete enough that another person could recognize the value in motion.
