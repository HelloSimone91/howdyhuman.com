Friendship

Category: Interpersonal

A strong bond between people that involves trust, support, and caring for each other.

Why this matters

Friendship matters when "A strong bond between people that involves trust, support, and caring for each other" becomes a practice someone can return to under pressure. It affects trust, conflict, repair, and the way people leave an exchange. One signal is the choice to connect when a more automatic reaction would be easier. The goal is to make friendship concrete enough that another person could recognize the value in motion.

Examples in action

  • Their friendship endured for decades through life's challenges, offering consistent support, honest feedback, and shared celebrations.
  • A repeated sign of friendship is the willingness to share even when the reward is delayed, private, or hard to measure.
  • In practice, friendship can show up inside a conversation where another person has something at stake: someone starts with the action behind "connect" instead of leaving the value as a private intention.

How to practice

  • When the situation gets blurry, return to the action words: connect, share, support, and enjoy.
  • Look for the place where friendship is being admired but not enacted, then choose the smallest useful action.
  • Find one real situation inside a conversation where another person has something at stake, then choose one action connected to "connect".
  • Write down the smallest possible version of the action behind "share" and use it to resist defensiveness.

Frequently asked questions

What does friendship mean as a value?
To value friendship is to let A strong bond between people that involves trust, support, and caring for each other guide behavior. The clearest clues are usually actions like connect, share, and support.
How do I practice friendship?
Practice friendship by choosing one action connected to "connect" and making it observable today. The goal is a real behavior, not a perfect description of the value.
What values are related to friendship?
Related values include Approachability, Camaraderie, and Charisma. Looking at them together can show whether the moment is asking for friendship, something adjacent, or a blend of several values.
