Perseverance

Category: Growth

Sticking with a task or goal even when it gets tough or takes a long time.

Why this matters

Perseverance matters because "Sticking with a task or goal even when it gets tough or takes a long time" can either stay abstract or become something people can observe. It becomes visible through adjustment, repetition, and honest feedback. It appears in the small verbs around it: persist, continue, and endure. That keeps perseverance from becoming decoration and turns it into something a person can actually practice.

Examples in action

  • After multiple failed prototypes over three years, the inventor's perseverance finally led to a working design that solved the problem.
  • When perseverance is working, the action usually leaves a trace: something gets protected, clarified, repaired, strengthened, or changed.
  • Perseverance can also appear through its neighboring values, such as Commitment, Determination, and Patience, when the situation asks someone to persist with more intention and precision.

How to practice

  • Name the pressure that makes perseverance harder, then choose one action that answers that pressure directly.
  • Make the value visible to one other person through a choice they could actually notice.
  • After acting, ask whether someone else could have observed perseverance in what you actually did.
  • When the situation gets blurry, return to the action words: persist, continue, endure, and overcome.

Frequently asked questions

What does perseverance mean as a value?
As a value, perseverance points to Sticking with a task or goal even when it gets tough or takes a long time. You can usually recognize it through concrete actions such as persist, continue, endure, and overcome.
How do I practice perseverance?
To practice perseverance, pick one of its verbs, such as "endure", and apply it to a specific conversation, decision, routine, or repair.
What values are related to perseverance?
Commitment, Determination, and Patience are useful companions to perseverance. They show where this value may need support, balance, or clearer boundaries.
