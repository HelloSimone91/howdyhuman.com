Interpersonal Values

Browse values in the Interpersonal category of the Howdy Human Dictionary of Values.

32 values in this category

Interpersonal value index

  • Appreciation Showing gratitude and recognizing the good things or people in life.
  • Approachability Being open and friendly to others, making it easy for them to talk and share their thoughts.
  • Camaraderie Building strong relationships and trust among people working together, creating a sense of friendship and support.
  • Caring Showing kindness and concern for others, helping them when they need support.
  • Charisma The ability to attract, influence, and inspire others through charm and confidence.
  • Collaboration Working together with others to achieve common goals and share ideas effectively.
  • Communication Sharing information clearly and respectfully with others to ensure understanding and connection.
  • Connection A strong bond between people that helps them understand and support each other.
  • Cooperation Working together toward shared goals.
  • Family A group of people related by blood, marriage, or adoption who support and care for each other.
  • Forgiveness Letting go of anger or resentment towards someone who has hurt you.
  • Friendship A strong bond between people that involves trust, support, and caring for each other.
  • Grace A kind of love and kindness that shows compassion and forgiveness to others, even when they don't deserve it.
  • Humor A way of thinking or expressing ideas that makes people laugh or feel good.
  • Kindness Being friendly and helpful to others, showing care and consideration in your actions and words.
  • Laughter A joyful expression often shared in response to humor or fun situations.
  • Leadership Guiding and inspiring others to work together towards common goals.
  • Love A deep feeling of affection and care for someone or something.
  • Loyalty Being loyal involves staying true to someone or something, supporting them through good times and bad.
  • Mentorship Helping others grow by sharing knowledge, experience, and support.
  • Networking Building and maintaining relationships with people to share information and opportunities.
  • Partnership Working together with others to achieve common goals and build strong relationships.
  • Respect Treating others with kindness and valuing their feelings, opinions, and rights.
  • Romance A deep emotional connection and attraction between people, often involving love and affection.
  • Selflessness Putting the needs and feelings of others before your own and helping them without expecting anything in return.
  • Sportsmanship Treating opponents with respect and fairness, showing graciousness in winning or losing.
  • Synergy Working together in a way that creates better results than individuals could achieve alone.
  • Teaching Helping others learn new things through guidance and sharing knowledge.
  • Teamwork Working together with others to achieve a common goal and support each other.
  • Thoughtfulness Showing care and consideration for others' feelings and needs in every action and decision.
  • Tolerance Respecting and accepting differences in others, even when you don't agree with them.
  • Trust Being honest and reliable with others creates a strong bond and encourages openness.
