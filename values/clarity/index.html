Clarity

Category: Interpersonal

Communicating and thinking in a way that is direct, understandable, and free from unnecessary confusion.

Example in action

During a tense meeting, Maya brought clarity by summarizing the decision in plain language, naming next steps, and checking that everyone understood before moving on.
