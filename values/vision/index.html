Vision

Category: Aspirations

Creating a clear and inspiring picture of the future that guides decisions and motivates people.

Why this matters

Vision matters when "Creating a clear and inspiring picture of the future that guides decisions and motivates people" has to become more than a sentence someone agrees with. As an aspiration, it gives direction to choices that would otherwise drift. It becomes concrete when someone chooses to imagine and lets that action carry the value. That is where the value earns trust: in the gap between what someone says matters and what they actually do.

Examples in action

  • The founder's vision of affordable healthcare for all communities guided the organization's growth and strategic partnerships for decades.
  • In practice, vision can show up when someone is deciding what future they are willing to build: someone starts with the action behind "imagine" instead of leaving the value as a private intention.
  • In a specific decision, vision becomes easier to recognize when the person can name what they are choosing to foresee and what that action serves.

How to practice

  • Find one real situation when someone is deciding what future they are willing to build, then choose one action connected to "imagine".
  • Notice where you talk about vision more easily than you practice it, then choose one concrete verb to close that gap.
  • Ask what vision would change in the next conversation, errand, decision, or repair.
  • Choose one related value, such as Creativity, Hope, and Foresight, and decide whether the moment needs that value, vision, or both.

Frequently asked questions

What does vision mean as a value?
Vision means Creating a clear and inspiring picture of the future that guides decisions and motivates people. It becomes a lived value when someone uses actions like imagine, envision, foresee, and conceptualize to make that meaning visible in a real situation.
How do I practice vision?
Start with a situation where short-term comfort is likely to take over. Then choose a small action connected to "envision" that makes vision visible.
What values are related to vision?
Creativity, Hope, and Foresight sit close to vision in the dictionary. They help frame the situations where this value becomes useful or complicated.
