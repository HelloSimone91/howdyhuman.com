Flexibility

Category: Mindset

Being open to change and able to adapt to new circumstances or challenges.

Why this matters

Flexibility matters when "Being open to change and able to adapt to new circumstances or challenges" has to become more than a sentence someone agrees with. As a mindset value, it changes how pressure, choice, and uncertainty are interpreted. It becomes concrete when someone chooses to adapt and lets that action carry the value. That is where the value earns trust: in the gap between what someone says matters and what they actually do.

Examples in action

  • When the outdoor event was threatened by rain, the coordinator showed flexibility by quickly creating a backup indoor plan that still met all objectives.
  • In practice, flexibility can show up under pressure, before the first reaction hardens into the whole story: someone starts with the action behind "adapt" instead of leaving the value as a private intention.
  • In a specific decision, flexibility becomes easier to recognize when the person can name what they are choosing to modify and what that action serves.

How to practice

  • Find one real situation under pressure, before the first reaction hardens into the whole story, then choose one action connected to "adapt".
  • Notice where you talk about flexibility more easily than you practice it, then choose one concrete verb to close that gap.
  • Ask what flexibility would change in the next conversation, errand, decision, or repair.
  • Choose one related value, such as Open-mindedness, Pragmatism, and Resilience, and decide whether the moment needs that value, flexibility, or both.

Frequently asked questions

What does flexibility mean as a value?
Flexibility means Being open to change and able to adapt to new circumstances or challenges. It becomes a lived value when someone uses actions like adapt, adjust, modify, and accommodate to make that meaning visible in a real situation.
How do I practice flexibility?
Start with a situation where old interpretation is likely to take over. Then choose a small action connected to "adjust" that makes flexibility visible.
What values are related to flexibility?
Open-mindedness, Pragmatism, and Resilience sit close to flexibility in the dictionary. They help frame the situations where this value becomes useful or complicated.
