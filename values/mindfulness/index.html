Mindfulness

Category: Mindset

Being aware of the present moment and accepting thoughts and feelings without judgment.

Example in action

During the high-pressure meeting, she practiced mindfulness by noticing her rising stress, taking deliberate breaths, and refocusing on the current discussion.
