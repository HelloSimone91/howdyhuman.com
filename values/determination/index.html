Determination

Category: Growth

Staying focused on your goals and not giving up, even when things get tough.

Why this matters

Determination matters because "Staying focused on your goals and not giving up, even when things get tough" can either stay abstract or become something people can observe. It belongs to the moments where trying again matters more than already being good. It appears in the small verbs around it: persist, resolve, and decide. That keeps determination from becoming decoration and turns it into something a person can actually practice.

Examples in action

  • After being rejected by multiple publishers, the author's determination kept her revising and submitting her manuscript until it was finally accepted.
  • One small example is a learning choice: using "decide" to make determination observable in one decision.
  • A repeated sign of determination is the willingness to resolve even when the reward is delayed, private, or hard to measure.

How to practice

  • Compare determination with Commitment, Patience, and Perseverance; notice where they support each other and where they ask for different actions.
  • When the situation gets blurry, return to the action words: persist, resolve, decide, and commit.
  • Turn the definition into a sentence that starts with "Today I will..." and includes one concrete behavior.
  • Make the value visible to one other person through a choice they could actually notice.

Frequently asked questions

What does determination mean as a value?
As a value, determination points to Staying focused on your goals and not giving up, even when things get tough. You can usually recognize it through concrete actions such as persist, resolve, decide, and commit.
How do I practice determination?
To practice determination, pick one of its verbs, such as "decide", and apply it to a specific conversation, decision, routine, or repair.
What values are related to determination?
Commitment, Patience, and Perseverance are useful companions to determination. They show where this value may need support, balance, or clearer boundaries.
