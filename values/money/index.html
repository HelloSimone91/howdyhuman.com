Money

Category: Aspirations

A medium of exchange used to buy goods and services.

Why this matters

Money matters when "A medium of exchange used to buy goods and services" becomes a practice someone can return to under pressure. This kind of value points attention toward a future someone is willing to help build. One signal is the choice to earn when a more automatic reaction would be easier. The goal is to make money concrete enough that another person could recognize the value in motion.

Examples in action

  • Rather than focusing only on accumulating money, she viewed it as a tool for creating security, supporting causes she valued, and providing opportunities.
  • A repeated sign of money is the willingness to invest even when the reward is delayed, private, or hard to measure.
  • In practice, money can show up when someone is deciding what future they are willing to build: someone starts with the action behind "earn" instead of leaving the value as a private intention.

How to practice

  • When the situation gets blurry, return to the action words: earn, invest, manage, and allocate.
  • Look for the place where money is being admired but not enacted, then choose the smallest useful action.
  • Find one real situation when someone is deciding what future they are willing to build, then choose one action connected to "earn".
  • Write down the smallest possible version of the action behind "invest" and use it to resist short-term comfort.

Frequently asked questions

What does money mean as a value?
To value money is to let A medium of exchange used to buy goods and services guide behavior. The clearest clues are usually actions like earn, invest, and manage.
How do I practice money?
Practice money by choosing one action connected to "earn" and making it observable today. The goal is a real behavior, not a perfect description of the value.
