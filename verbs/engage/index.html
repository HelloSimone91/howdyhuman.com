Verb: engage

This page collects values that are commonly lived through the action engage.

12 related values

  • Approachability — Being open and friendly to others, making it easy for them to talk and share their thoughts.
  • Charisma — The ability to attract, influence, and inspire others through charm and confidence.
  • Commitment — Being dedicated to doing what you said you would do and supporting others in achieving their goals.
  • Communication — Sharing information clearly and respectfully with others to ensure understanding and connection.
  • Community — A group of people who share common interests, goals, or values and support each other.
  • Connection — A strong bond between people that helps them understand and support each other.
  • Fun — Enjoyment and amusement that brings joy and entertainment to people's lives.
  • Movement — The act of changing position or location, often involving physical activity or effort.
  • Networking — Building and maintaining relationships with people to share information and opportunities.
  • Presence — Being fully engaged and attentive in the moment, showing awareness of one's surroundings and the people in it.
  • Sports — Activities that involve physical exertion and skill, often played individually or in teams for competition or enjoyment.
  • Willingness — A readiness to do what is needed or to accept new ideas and experiences.
