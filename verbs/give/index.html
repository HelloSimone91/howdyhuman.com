Verb: give

This page collects values that are commonly lived through the action give.

5 related values

  • Benevolence — Showing kindness and helping others without expecting anything in return.
  • Kindness — Being friendly and helpful to others, showing care and consideration in your actions and words.
  • Philanthropy — Helping others by giving money, time, or resources to support causes that improve people's lives and communities.
  • Selflessness — Putting the needs and feelings of others before your own and helping them without expecting anything in return.
  • Service — Helping others by providing assistance, support, or care.
