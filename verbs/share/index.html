  • Cooperation — Working together toward shared goals.
  • Empathy — Understanding and sharing the feelings of others to connect and support them better.
  • Friendship — A strong bond between people that involves trust, support, and caring for each other.
  • Honesty — Being truthful and transparent in thoughts, words, and actions without hiding or lying.
  • Humor — A way of thinking or expressing ideas that makes people laugh or feel good.
  • Kindness — Being friendly and helpful to others, showing care and consideration in your actions and words.
  • Laughter — A joyful expression often shared in response to humor or fun situations.
  • Mentorship — Helping others grow by sharing knowledge, experience, and support.
  • Partnership — Working together with others to achieve common goals and build strong relationships.
  • Philanthropy — Helping others by giving money, time, or resources to support causes that improve people's lives and communities.
  • Romance — A deep emotional connection and attraction between people, often involving love and affection.
  • Self Expression — The ability to share thoughts, feelings, and ideas openly and honestly.
