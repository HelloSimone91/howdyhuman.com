Verb: endure

This page collects values that are commonly lived through the action endure.

6 related values

  • Determination — Staying focused on your goals and not giving up, even when things get tough.
  • Longevity — Prioritizing a long, healthy, and meaningful life through sustainable habits and choices.
  • Patience — The ability to wait calmly and without frustration for something to happen or for a situation to change.
  • Perseverance — Sticking with a task or goal even when it gets tough or takes a long time.
  • Resilience — The ability to bounce back from difficulties and keep going despite challenges.
