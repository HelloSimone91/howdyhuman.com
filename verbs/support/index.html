Verb: support

This page collects values that are commonly lived through the action support.

21 related values

  • Advocacy — Speaking up for what is right and supporting others to create positive change in the community.
  • Benevolence — Showing kindness and helping others without expecting anything in return.
  • Camaraderie — Building strong relationships and trust among people working together, creating a sense of friendship and support.
  • Caring — Showing kindness and concern for others, helping them when they need support.
  • Comfort — A state of physical and emotional ease that makes people feel relaxed and safe.
  • Community — A group of people who share common interests, goals, or values and support each other.
  • Compassion — Caring for others and wanting to help them when they are suffering or in need.
  • Cooperation — Working together toward shared goals.
  • Family — A group of people related by blood, marriage, or adoption who support and care for each other.
  • Friendship — A strong bond between people that involves trust, support, and caring for each other.
  • Justice — Fair treatment for everyone, ensuring that rights are respected and wrongs are corrected.
  • Kindness — Being friendly and helpful to others, showing care and consideration in your actions and words.
  • Love — A deep feeling of affection and care for someone or something.
  • Loyalty — Being loyal involves staying true to someone or something, supporting them through good times and bad.
  • Mental Health — A state of well-being where individuals can cope with stress, work productively, and contribute to their community.
  • Mentorship — Helping others grow by sharing knowledge, experience, and support.
  • Philanthropy — Helping others by giving money, time, or resources to support causes that improve people's lives and communities.
  • Self-Compassion — Being kind and understanding to yourself during tough times or when you make mistakes.
  • Selflessness — Putting the needs and feelings of others before your own and helping them without expecting anything in return.
  • Service — Helping others by providing assistance, support, or care.
  • Teamwork — Working together with others to achieve a common goal and support each other.
